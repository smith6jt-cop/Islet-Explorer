  A suggested supplemental figure caption:

  Supplemental Figure X. scVI model comparison: effect of Age/Gender categorical covariates on latent space quality. The original model (blue) included
  Age and Gender as categorical covariates, which uniquely identify each of the 15 nPOD donors. (A) PCA of the islet-level latent space colored by donor
  identity shows improved donor mixing in the original model. (B) Disease status (ND/Aab+/T1D) separation is preserved in both models. (C) Per-dimension
  eta² reveals that the no-covariate model allocates substantially more latent variance to donor identity rather than disease biology. (D) Distribution of
   per-islet donor silhouette scores; lower values indicate better donor mixing. (E) Fraction of k=15 nearest neighbors from the same donor; dashed line
  indicates random expectation. (F) Summary of key metrics; asterisk indicates the better model. (G,H) Pseudotime gradient along PCA axes; the original
  model shows a smooth disease-progression gradient along PC0, while the no-covariate model buries this signal under donor effects.
